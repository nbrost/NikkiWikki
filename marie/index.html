Madam Flora Cruz

Biography

Madam Flora Cruz, a 42-year-old anesthesiologist, leads a double life. By day, she is a respected healthcare professional, known for her expertise in administering anesthesia. However, behind closed doors, she engages in a lucrative and illicit side business, siphoning off excess vials of anesthesia and selling them on the black market. Madam Cruz's clientele ranges from thrill-seekers to those with nefarious intentions. She maintains a network of contacts in the criminal underworld, always staying one step ahead of the authorities. Despite the risks, she finds a strange thrill in leading this double life, justifying her actions by the financial rewards it brings. However, beneath the surface, Madam Cruz is haunted by the moral implications of her choices. She knows that her actions could have devastating consequences for both her patients and those who use the stolen anesthesia. The weight of her guilt grows heavier each day, but the lure of the black market keeps her entangled in this dangerous web. As she navigates the complexities of her dual existence, Madam Flora Cruz must constantly be on guard, knowing that one misstep could expose her secret and bring her carefully constructed world crashing down. She lives with the knowledge that her secret could be revealed at any moment, risking her career, her freedom, and the trust of those who depend on her.
